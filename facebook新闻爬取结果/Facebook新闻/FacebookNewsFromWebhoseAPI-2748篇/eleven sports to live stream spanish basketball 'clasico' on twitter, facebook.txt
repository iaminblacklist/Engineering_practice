Eleven Sports to live stream Spanish basketball 'Clasico' on Twitter, Facebook Friday 10 November 2017 | 15:09 CET | News UK-based TV group Eleven Sports has announced an agreement with Spanish basketball association ACB to live stream the Real Madrid versus FC Barcelona 'Clasico' game on Facebook and Twitter on 12 November. The deal marks the first time the ACB Liga Endesa fixture will be streamed live on Facebook and Twitter across a number of Eleven Sports markets, namely Belgium, Italy, Singapore, Taiwan and the US.
