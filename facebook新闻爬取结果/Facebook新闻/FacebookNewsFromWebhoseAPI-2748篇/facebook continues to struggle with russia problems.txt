Wochit News   15:35 CET 
Facebook, along with other web platforms, such as Twitter and Google, are facing hearings this week that will probe into the ways their platforms were exploited. Not only will they be talking about how Russian bought online ads may have helped sway voters in the 2016 elections but they will also dig into the problem of foreign actors using fake account and deliberately misinforming the public. Just this Friday, top Senate Judiciary Committee Democrat Dianne Feinstein asked Facebook CEO Mark Zuckerberg additional data about Russian activity on its networks
