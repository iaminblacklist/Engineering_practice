Home / Business / Facebook quarterly profit leaps 79 per cent to USD 4.7 billion Facebook quarterly profit leaps 79 per cent to USD 4.7 billion — By Agencies | Nov 02, 2017 08:11 am FOLLOW US: San Francisco , Facebook today reported profits leapt 79 per cent on booming revenue from online ads in the third quarter, topping investor forecasts and buoying shares already at record highs. 
The leading social network said it made a profit o USD 4.7 billion in the quarter that ended on September 30, compared with USD 2.6 billion in the same period a year earlier.
