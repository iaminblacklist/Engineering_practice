Facebook Inc. (FB) shares ticked up in the extended session Wednesday after the social networking company beat earnings and revenue expectations. Facebook shares rose 2.2% to $186.76 after hours. The company reported third-quarter net income of $4.7 billion, or $1.59 a share, compared to $2.63 billion, or 90 cents a share, in the year-ago period. Revenue rose to $10.33 billion from $7 billion in the year-ago period. Analysts surveyed by FactSet had estimated $1.28 a share on revenue of $9.85 billion. Daily active users, a metric closely watched by analysts and investors, rose 16% to 1.37 billion, compared with the year-earlier period. For the fourth quarter, analysts model earnings of $1.70 a share on revenue of $12 billion. Facebook stock has gained 58.9% this year, with the S&P 500 index rising 15%.
