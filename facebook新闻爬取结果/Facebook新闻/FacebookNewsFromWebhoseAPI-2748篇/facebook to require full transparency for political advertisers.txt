Facebook announced Friday that advertisers running federal election-related ads in the United States would be required to verify their identitym as well as run disclosures on each ad. The move comes on the heels of Facebook CEO Mark Zuckerberg outlining initial steps that the company was taking to help protect the integrity of election. Facebook's play follows Twitter saying that it would publicly disclose all ads on its platform, along with the identity of who purchased them earlier this week.
