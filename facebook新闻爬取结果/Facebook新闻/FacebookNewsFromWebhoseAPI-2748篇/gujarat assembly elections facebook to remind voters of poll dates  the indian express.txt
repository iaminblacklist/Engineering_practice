The Election Commission has tied up with Facebook for increasing voter participation in the upcoming Assembly elections in Gujarat. As part of this collaboration, Facebook will promote a reminder in news feed of people’s Facebook page about the two phases of elections to be held on December 9 and 14. 
The reminder will help encourage participation in the elections, and will run in conjunction with the campaign run by the office of the chief electoral officer of Gujarat to encourage people to vote.
