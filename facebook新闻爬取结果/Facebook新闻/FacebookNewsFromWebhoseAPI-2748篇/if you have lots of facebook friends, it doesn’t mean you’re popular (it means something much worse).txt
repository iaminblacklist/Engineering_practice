People who are materialistic also have an urge to compare themselves with other people on Facebook. Researchers from Ruhr-University Bochum conducted online questionnaires with 242 Facebook users. ‘Materialistic people use Facebook more frequently because they tend to objectify their Facebook friends – they acquire Facebook friends to increase their possession,’ said lead author Phillip Ozimek. ‘Facebook provides the perfect platform for social comparisons, with millions of profiles and information about people.
