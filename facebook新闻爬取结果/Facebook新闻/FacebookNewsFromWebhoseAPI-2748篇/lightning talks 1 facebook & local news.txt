Lightning talks 1: Facebook & local news Huffduffed by jameshatts on November 1st, 2017 
Lightning talks are quick, focused presentations. Dorrine Mendoza and Josh Mabry of Facebook will talk about Facebook’s efforts to work with local news publishers and try to dispel the mystery of the algorithm that controls the platform’s news feed. Reynolds Journalism Institute fellow Christopher Guess will demo "Push," an open-source mobile app he’s designed for small news organizations. Dylan Smile, publisher of TucsonSentinel.com and chairman of the LION board of directors, will talk about the organization’s growth and future plans.
