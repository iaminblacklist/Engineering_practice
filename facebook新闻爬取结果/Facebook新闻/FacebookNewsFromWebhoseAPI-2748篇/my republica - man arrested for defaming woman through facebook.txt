Man arrested for defaming woman through Facebook 
October 30, 2017 15:16 PM Republica | Shares 
CHITWAN, Oct 30: Police have arrested a man on a charge of defaming a woman by uploading her photos on her fake Facebook accounts he created himself. 
A complaint lodged by the victim has named Nabin Timilsina, 27, of Bakaiya rural municipality-4 in Makawanpur as involved in her character assassinating by posting her pictures and contact number on her fake Facebook ID, with the status calling for interested to contact with her for pornographic activities, said Deputy Superintendent of Police, Shiva Sharma Acharya.
