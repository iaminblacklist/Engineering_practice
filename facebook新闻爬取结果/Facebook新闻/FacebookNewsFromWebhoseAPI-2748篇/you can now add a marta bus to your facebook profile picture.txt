Atlanta has reached peak MARTA meme mayhem.
After a Weather Channel feed captured a MARTA bus rolling into frame and hitting the brakes at the same time of the Georgia Dome’s implosion, the phenomenon has been discussed by Stephen Colbert , shown on the Today Show and mocked by the Falcons and Hawks.
The next logical step? Well, someone who goes by Henry Stinson Birdseye III created a Facebook frame to make that bus crash your profile picture.
